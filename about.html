Our Story

Driven By Innovation & Excellence

Synergetic Solutions (Pvt) Ltd is Sri Lanka's premier destination for comprehensive IT equipment sales. We bridge the gap between world-class technology manufacturers and local consumers by providing top-tier solutions ranging from workstations to networking gear.

We are authorized retailers and experts in top global brands including HP, DELL, LENOVO, ASUS, ACER, CANON, TOSHIBA, and DINTEC. Furthermore, we are leaders in consumable supplies, providing both Original components (HP, Epson, Canon) and highly reliable Compatible supplies (Eco Plus, Asta) ensuring our clients are always running seamlessly.
